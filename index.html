Welcome to Realm of Shadows: Castle Conundrum!

You're an adventurer belonging to a guild, as the strongest adventurer in the guild, you have been tasked to explore an ancient castle newly found by scouts. However, once you enter, the castle doors slam shut!

You're trapped in the castle! Explore the castle, find items and escape!

The commands usable are 'north', 'east', 'south', 'west' and 'use [item]'!
